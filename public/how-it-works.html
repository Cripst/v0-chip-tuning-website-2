How It Works

Get your professionally tuned ECU file in three simple steps. Fast, secure, and reliable.

1. Upload Your File

Read the original ECU file from your customer's vehicle using any compatible tool and upload it through our secure portal.

2. Our Engineers Work

Our certified engineers analyze your file and apply the requested modifications using professional-grade tools and calibrations.

3. Download & Flash

Receive your tuned file via email notification, download it from your account, and flash it to the vehicle. Done!

Step 1

Upload Your Original File

Use your preferred ECU reading tool (KESS, KTAG, Autotuner, etc.) to extract the original ECU file from your customer's vehicle. Our portal accepts all common file formats.

  • Secure 256-bit SSL encrypted upload
  • Supports .bin, .ori, .mod, .fls, .hex files
  • Specify vehicle details and requirements
  • Instant confirmation and ticket number
Step 2

Expert Calibration

Our team of certified ECU engineers uses professional-grade software like WinOLS to carefully modify your file. Every calibration is tailored to your specific vehicle and hardware configuration.

  • Certified, experienced engineers
  • Professional-grade tuning software
  • Hardware-specific calibrations
  • Quality assurance checks
Step 3

Download & Flash

Once your file is ready, you'll receive an email notification. Download the tuned file from your dashboard and flash it to the vehicle using your reading tool. It's that simple!

  • Average 30-minute turnaround
  • Email and SMS notifications
  • Unlimited free revisions
  • 24/7 technical support
